cpx.yaml looks like:

presets:
	cf/c++20:
		c: g++ -O2 -std=c++20 -o % %.cpp
		r: ./%
	cf/c++23:
		c: g++ -O2 -std=c++23 -o % %.cpp
		r: ./%
	c++: cf/c++23
targets:
	sol.cpp:
		e: cf/c++20
		+c: -U=U

then commands look like:

cpx bundle sol.cpp
cpx compile sol.cpp
cpx run sol.cpp
cpx submit [url] sol.cpp

this makes things speedy and as "single source of truth" as
possible
